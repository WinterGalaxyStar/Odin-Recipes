Tunnel Snakes Rule

These here Lasangas saved my life when running from the tunnel snakes

Ingredients

  • One Tunnel Snakes Kids Skin
  • Lots of Cheese scrapped from your toe
  • Blood of your enemies
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Murder the nearest Tunnel Snake to you
  2. Preheat your oven to 5000 degrees celsius
  3. drain the blood from your murdered tunnel snake
  4. remove all toe cheese and slapped on that pan
  5. combine all ingredients into a pan that will fit
  6. cook for 2 seconds and serve
